Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Middle-earth Explorer works completely offline. All map data, events, and character information are included in the app.

Is the app really free?

Yes. Middle-earth Explorer is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions.

Does the app collect any data?

No. We collect zero data. Your bookmarks and preferences stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my bookmarks?

Bookmarks are stored locally on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, bookmarks will be lost. We recommend keeping a backup of your device through iCloud or iTunes.

The app is not available in my language

Middle-earth Explorer currently supports 31 languages. If your language is not included, please reach out and let us know — we're always looking to expand.
