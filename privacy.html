Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Middle-earth Explorer is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app qualifies for Apple's "Data Not Collected" privacy label.

Data Collection

Middle-earth Explorer does not collect any data from you. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs
  • No cookies or web beacons

Data Storage

All app data — including your bookmarks, collections, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Middle-earth Explorer does not integrate any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, Middle-earth Explorer is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
